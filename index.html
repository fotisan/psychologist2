Meet Dr. Carter

Thoughtful, collaborative care that respects your pace and your story.

I’m a licensed psychologist specializing in anxiety, perfectionism, and relationship concerns. Many of my clients are high-achieving professionals who feel like they are “holding it all together” on the outside while feeling overwhelmed or stuck inside.

In our work together, we slow things down. We look at your patterns with curiosity rather than judgment, and we build practical tools that you can take back into your daily life. My approach is warm, grounded, and science-informed, drawing from CBT, ACT, and mindfulness-based therapies.

Therapy is not about “fixing” you. It’s about creating enough space and support so that you can move toward a life that feels more aligned, connected, and sustainable.

  • PhD in Clinical Psychology · University of Washington
  • 10+ years of experience in private practice and university counseling centers
  • Special focus on anxiety, burnout, and complex relationship patterns

How I can support you

Flexible, individualized care for the seasons of life when you need more than just “pushing through.”

Individual Therapy

Weekly or bi-weekly sessions focused on anxiety, burnout, self-criticism, and life transitions. Together we develop coping skills, emotional awareness, and a more compassionate inner voice.

  • Anxiety & stress
  • Perfectionism & overthinking
  • Life transitions & identity shifts

Couples Therapy

Support for partners navigating conflict, distance, or major decisions. We work on communication patterns, emotional safety, and rebuilding connection.

  • Communication & conflict
  • Trust & intimacy
  • Life changes & parenting stress

Anxiety & Panic Treatment

Structured, evidence-based treatment for generalized anxiety, social anxiety, and panic. We combine CBT, exposure, and mindfulness tools at a pace that feels manageable.

  • Generalized anxiety
  • Panic attacks & health anxiety
  • Performance & social anxiety

Not sure which service fits? We can decide together during your free 15-minute consultation.
